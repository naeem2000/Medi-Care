Medi-Care



Getting your doctor and medication, in the safety of your home.

Benefits of using Medi-Care



Much Easier Access to Healthcare

Medi-Care can be of great use for people who live in distant, rural areas. In absence of healthcare facilities nearby, patients can have their healthcare consultation directly via this web-app. All they need is a smartphone with a working internet connection.


Real time Treatment advice


Medi-Care allows patients to ask anything related to their health from hospital staff-nurses, doctors. Patients can take remote advice related to drug schedule, emergency help etc. via a text from the web-app or can even send the voice note.


Save a Lot on Healthcare Costs


Medi-Care offers patients the liberty of connecting to any healthcare service without physically visiting the hospital. This saves both your time and transportation cost.


Convenient Access to Electronic Health Records


Medi-Care keeps EHRs (electronic health records), so that doctors could access them on demand. A single place for a patient’s clinical records saves the doctor’s time.
